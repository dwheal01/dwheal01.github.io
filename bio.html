Biography

I was born in Washington D.C. on September 29th, 1997. From the years 2002-2008 I attended Rock Creek Forest Elementary School (a language immersion school) where I learned Spanish. Within these years I learned to swim, ice skate, ski, and play tennis.

From the years 2008-2011 I attended Westland Middle school. During this time I learned to ride horses, began to travel the world, and started to discover my strength in math and science.

2011-2015 were my High School Years. I went to Bethesda Chevey Chase High School. Here I was a member of the Girls Varsity Tennis Team and the Mock Trial Team. I took my first physics class which inspired me to become an engineer.

In 2015 I started my college education at Tufts University. I declared a major in Electrical Engineering my first year, and soon after declared a minor in computer science. I joined the Tufts Ski Racing Team and Studied abroad in Talloires France.

I am not a senior, set to graduate with May, 2019. This summer I will be moving to San Diego, CA to work as a Software Engineer at Qualcomm.
